About the Workshop

This One Day Workshop on Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) was sponsored by UPCST-DST and organized by the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering, Institute of Engineering & Technology, Bundelkhand University, Jhansi on 20th February 2026.

The workshop aimed at building and creating awareness for protecting creativity and intellectual property rights among students, researchers, and faculty members. Distinguished speakers from leading institutions shared their expertise on patent filing, IPR laws, and innovation protection.

The workshop was patronized by Prof. Mukesh Pandey, Vice Chancellor of Bundelkhand University, Jhansi, and featured expert sessions by renowned speakers in the field of IPR and innovation.
